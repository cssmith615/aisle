Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up the AI Assistant?

Go to the AI Assistant screen and tap the key icon in the header. You'll be prompted to enter your Anthropic API key. You can get one at console.anthropic.com. Your key is stored securely on your device and never shared with us.

How does the Wedding Party share code work?

From the Wedding Party screen, add a member and tap their card to see their unique share code. Share it with them — they can use it to log in at the "Join as Wedding Party" option on the sign-in screen and see their assigned tasks. No app account required.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same account on any device and all your wedding data will sync automatically.

How do I switch between multiple weddings/events?

From the Dashboard, tap the event name in the hero card. A switcher will appear letting you select an existing event or create a new one. (Premium required for more than 1 event.)

How do I export my guest list or budget?

In the Guest List screen, tap the share icon in the top right to export a CSV file. In the Budget or Day-of Timeline screens, tap the share icon to export a PDF. You can then email, print, or save the file from the share sheet.

What is the Pro tier?

The Pro tier is designed for professional wedding planners managing multiple client weddings. It unlocks the Clients dashboard where you can add client events and manage their checklist, budget, vendors, and guests separately.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app:

  • Open Stefana and go to Profile (tap the ⚙ gear icon on the Dashboard)
  • Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account"
  • Confirm the deletion — your account and all wedding data will be permanently removed immediately
